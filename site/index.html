Overview

Built like a platform, explained like a product

The goal is not to build another crypto dashboard. The goal is to prove platform thinking: ingestion, normalization, features, narrative logic, interpretable outputs, and a public-facing layer that still feels coherent to non-technical reviewers.

Product Positioning

Closer to a research operating layer than a chart page

The system is designed to help prioritize which assets and narratives deserve investigation, not to pretend that one fragile model can predict price direction in isolation.

Why It Matters

Crypto signals live across multiple market surfaces

Spot, derivatives, and on-chain behavior often tell different parts of the same story. This project organizes them into a cleaner analytical layer with explicit drivers and regime tags.
